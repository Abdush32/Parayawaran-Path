We are pioneers in the electric mobility revolution, dedicated to transforming urban transportation in India through cutting-edge wireless charging technology. Our innovative system, based on the installation of cables beneath road surfaces, allows electric vehicles to charge seamlessly while driving. By eliminating the need for traditional charging stations and reducing downtime, our solution provides unmatched convenience, enabling a more sustainable and efficient approach to powering electric vehicles. Driven by our vision of creating smarter, greener cities, we are committed to reducing environmental impact, fostering the adoption of electric mobility, and redefining the way people experience transportation. Through relentless innovation and collaboration, we aim to shape the future of mobility, making it wireless, sustainable, and accessible for everyone.

Our mission is to revolutionize urban mobility in India by providing innovative, sustainable, and seamless wireless charging solutions for electric vehicles. By integrating cutting-edge technology with infrastructure, we aim to empower a future where vehicles can charge effortlessly while driving, reducing reliance on traditional charging methods, minimizing downtime, and enhancing convenience. Through this transformative approach, we strive to accelerate the adoption of electric mobility, reduce environmental impact, and contribute to building smarter, greener cities.

What We Do at Paryawaran Path

Innovative Wireless Charging Technology

We install advanced charging cables beneath road surfaces, allowing vehicles to charge on the go, eliminating the need for stationary charging points and saving time for users.

Promoting Sustainability

By encouraging the adoption of electric vehicles with convenient charging options, we aim to reduce carbon emissions and contribute to cleaner air and a greener planet.

Driving Electric Mobility Adoption

Through our cutting-edge systems, we make electric vehicles more practical and appealing, accelerating their adoption and supporting the transition to sustainable transportation.

Smart Infrastructure Development

Our solutions integrate seamlessly with urban infrastructure, supporting smarter city development and modernizing transportation systems in Indian cities.
